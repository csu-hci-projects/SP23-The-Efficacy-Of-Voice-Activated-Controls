Welcome to the voice test

You will notice the game interface has changed slightly. The game will now be played by using your microphone described in the beginning of the experiment. Please reduce background noise and be sure to speak audibly while articulating as best as possible to avoid error. You will press start just as you did in the first step, except now you will have to press record and say the commands "one", "two", "three", or "four". If no speech is detected, your browser will alert you and nothing will be registered. All you have to do is press record and try again. If you for some reason dont record an entire sequence and fall short a command, you can click record again and pick up where you left off. If you speak to quickly and to not articulate each word, the voice recognition can run together and not register a command.
