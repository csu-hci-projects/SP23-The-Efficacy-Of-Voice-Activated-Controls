Your first and last name will be collected during registration but it is only to generate a unique ID and will not be used in any data analysis after collection. By using this application and registering your information, you consent to the use of your experiment data for research purposes. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at jgoing@colostate.edu. Thank you for your participation.
